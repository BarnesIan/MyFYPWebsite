Title:

Malverticus Shield: Safeguarding the Digital World from Malvertising and Malicious URLs

Project Description:

Malverticus is a web-based software module designed to address the growing threats of malvertising and malicious URLs in the digital landscape. This innovative cybersecurity solution offers both academic and commercial value by providing proactive defence mechanisms against these prevalent threats.
